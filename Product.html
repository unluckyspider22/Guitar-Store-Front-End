    Taylor is an American musical instrument brand, founded in 1970 by artists such as Dave Matthews, Tony Iommi, Jason Mraz, and Taylor Swift. Taylor's guitars fit the tone of their songs, the way they played their music at the time.

    Initially, the company only produced pure acoustic plants, then they added the T5 hollowbody and the electric hybrid semi-hollowbody. The electric guitar produced in 2005 increased the versatility of the performer, the creativity of the performers, and the variety of styles that made them the most innovative guitar manufacturer in the world.

  • The Taylor Guitar brand is known for its distinctive bright sound quality with a sharp clarity that is rare in pure guitar.
